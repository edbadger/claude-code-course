## PRD opening (Enterprise AI Controls)

**Context.** Enterprise renewals are stalling on AI data concerns. Three renewals this quarter named it as the primary blocker. Security has signed off on the architecture. What's missing is the customer-facing controls.

**Problem.** Enterprise admins have no way to control what Miro AI touches. They can turn AI on or off at the workspace level, and that's it. No template-level opt-out, no data residency selection, no audit trail.

**Proposal.** Ship three controls: per-workspace data residency, template-level opt-out, and audit logging with 90-day retention. Trade-off: the mobile AI exploration slips to Q3.
