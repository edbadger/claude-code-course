# Goals

Up to 3 strategic goals for the quarter. Edit at quarter boundaries. Re-read before any planning session.

Each goal follows this shape:

- `## Goal N: [title]` — short, action-oriented
- `Why this matters:` — one sentence on the strategic reason
- `Metric:` — how you'll measure it, with a deadline

---

## Q2 2026

## Goal 1: Lift trial-to-paid conversion from 18% to 25%
Why this matters: Conversion is the biggest revenue lever we have without paying for more top-of-funnel traffic.
Metric: 25% trial-to-paid rate sustained for 4 consecutive weeks by 30 June 2026.

## Goal 2: Ship the self-serve onboarding redesign to all users
Why this matters: Current onboarding drops 40% of trials in the first session; the redesign is our single biggest bet on the conversion goal.
Metric: 100% rollout with no guardrail regressions by 1 June 2026.

## Goal 3: Establish a weekly customer discovery rhythm
Why this matters: Roadmap decisions have been drifting from evidence; a standing discovery rhythm keeps us honest.
Metric: 5 customer interviews per week, synthesised every Friday, through Q2.
