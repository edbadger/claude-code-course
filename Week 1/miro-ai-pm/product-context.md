# Product Context

I'm a PM on the Miro AI team. This file is the default context Claude should load when I ask anything about my work.

## What Miro AI is
A layer of AI features inside the Miro canvas. The main ones are sticky note clustering, diagram generation from prompts, board summarisation, and meeting transcript ingestion. Not a separate product. Users hit it mid-flow.

## Users
- **Primary:** active Miro users in product, design, research, and ops teams. They arrive with a meeting transcript, a pile of sticky notes, or a blank board, and want a first draft faster.
- **Secondary:** enterprise admins who care about what data the AI touches and where it goes.

## Key metrics
- **AI 30-day retention.** Target 55%. Currently 44%.
- **AI sessions per active AI-user per week.** Currently 2.1, flat for 8 weeks.
- **Workflow completion rate** (new metric, April 2026): share of AI sessions where the user keeps the AI output on the board. Currently 38%.
- **Enterprise opt-in:** share of enterprise workspaces with AI enabled. 61%.

## Stage
Growth, but uneven. Early adoption came from curiosity. Retention is the open question. Most features still feel like "try once, go back to manual". Q2 is about closing that gap.

## Competitive context
- **Figma AI** (shipped late 2025). Customers ask why our clustering is slower and less accurate. It's the comparison that comes up most.
- **Microsoft Loop.** Weaker AI, deeper enterprise trust. Shows up in renewal conversations, not feature ones.
