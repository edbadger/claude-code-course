# Goals

Top priorities for Q2 2026 (April to June). Three goals. Everything else is secondary. Re-read before planning sessions.

---

## Goal 1: Lift AI 30-day retention from 44% to 55%
Why this matters: Adoption is fine, retention is the bottleneck. Without this, the whole AI investment looks like a novelty.
Metric: 55% of users who try an AI feature use one again within 30 days, sustained for 4 weeks, by 30 June 2026.
Status: Behind. Currently 44%, up from 42% at start of quarter. Needs to move 2.5 points per month.
Biggest lever: Workflow Starter (see current-projects.md). If that works, retention follows. If not, we re-plan in May.

## Goal 2: Ship Enterprise AI Controls to 5 reference customers
Why this matters: Enterprise renewals keep stalling on AI data concerns. Named references unblock sales and give us real deployment feedback.
Metric: 5 enterprise workspaces live on the new controls, willing to be named in sales conversations, by 30 June 2026.
Status: On track. Three committed (HSBC, Deloitte, Nestle), two in conversation. Controls shipping 15 May.
Owner: me and Chris Taylor (sales).

## Goal 3: AI Eval Platform live as model release gate
Why this matters: We've shipped two quality regressions in the past quarter that we only caught post-launch. No more.
Metric: No model update ships without an eval regression check. Dashboard used weekly by the team. By 30 June 2026.
Status: On track. Internal launch 20 May.
Owner: Ana Lopez (data science).

- Meeting bot integrations (marketing keeps asking, still not a priority)
